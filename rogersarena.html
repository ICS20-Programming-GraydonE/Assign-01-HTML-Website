Rogers Arena

Rogers Arena is a multi-purpose arena located in downtown Vancouver and is the home of the Vancouver Canucks. It was opened in 1995 and was formally known as General Motors Place until 2010. It is also the home of the Vancouver Warriors Lacross Team and was previously the home of the Vancouver Grizzlies of the NBA.
